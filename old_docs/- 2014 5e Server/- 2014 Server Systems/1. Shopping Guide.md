# Shopping Guide
## The Starry Shore Shopping Guide

### Buying Mundane items
Characters can buy basic, mundane items (such as armor, weapons, and adventuring gear) that are listed in the Player‚Äôs Handbook (PHB) for the price listed in the PHB.

This only needs to be logged in the character tracking sheet. Characters can also sell these items for half of the price listed in the PHB.

Some mundane items have prices listed in the Mundane Items tab.

### Buying Magic items
DMs may permit players to discover shops within their worlds, either during live sessions or through text-based roleplay (TRP), and purchase items from these lists found in other tabs. In these shops, players can purchase magical and mundane items.

Shops are permitted to charge more than the cost in the Cart but cannot charge less. DMs retain full discretion over the availability of items, including what items are available and in what quantities. If the items are not listed in the cart, they will be considered homebrew within The Starry Shore.

<div style="border-left: 4px solid #e67e22; background: #fff3e0; padding: 1em; border-radius: 6px; color: #000;">
<strong> Characters are only permitted to buy items that are their Tier or lower.</strong>
</div>

For players seeking to purchase items, there are two methods:

1.  During a live session, if the DM allows it, players can submit a list of items they wish to purchase.
2.  Through shops in the market district category or any other shops within TRP worlds, as well as VIP buildings. Players engage in TRP interactions to procure items and then send the list to the DM.

### Selling Magic Items via live session or TRP
Selling an item at 50% of its base gold value is free and does not require a check. It must be logged by a DM in the <span style="display:inline-block;
              background-color:#4b4d53;
              color:#ffffff;
              border-radius:6px;
              padding:4px 10px;
              font-family:'gg sans','Segoe UI',sans-serif;
              font-size:0.9em;
              font-weight:500;
              user-select:none;">#magic-item-log
</span>.

Selling an item at 100% of its base gold value requires a check and costs DTP. The check is a Charisma (Persuasion) check with a DC equal to the item‚Äôs tier x 5. The DTP cost is also equal to the item‚Äôs tier x 5.

Buffs on the selling roll in live sessions are the same as crafting ones.
