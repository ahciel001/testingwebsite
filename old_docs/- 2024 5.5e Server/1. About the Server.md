## How Things Work here?
Player Characters (PCs) exist in The Starry Shore, an interplanar hub with the main street leading to many portals. Dungeon Masters create those portals leading to whatever worlds they dream up. PCs can visit those worlds via live sessions and/or text role play (trp). PCs earn levels and gold via hours played in live sessions. Characters continue to level past level 20 using the hours-played formula. Though their level will not increase any further, they will gain additional rewards for each level: 

* 21, 22, 23: An Epic Boon each
* 24: The ability to earn an artifact rarity item for your character 
* 25-30: A +2 increase to one ability score and its maximum.

## Modes of Play:
1. **Live Voice Sessions:** You gain "hours played", gold, and items as rewards from playing. (We use "hours played" in place of experience for leveling up.) This is the **main** method of play. 
   And these kind of sessions can be anything, from lighthearted and funny to dark and serious.
   
2. **Text Roleplay:** or TRP, there's 2 types of it:
 - PBP/TRP in DMs worlds: This for casual trp, you can role play whenever you're free and the dm does the same.
 - Trp for fun: This is the trp that happens inside the starry shore (Crossroad category, Starry City category)

## Member Responsibilities
*   Have common sense, and follow the Server Code of Conduct.
*   You must be 18 years of age or older to play on the server (This is both for social and legal reasons. Sorry, no exceptions.).
*   PVP requires consent from both parties including the DM. Consent needs to be communicated, not just implied.
*   Sexual themes are not allowed until consent from the whole party is given.
*   Adult themes and language may appear. Our DM worlds' range from lighthearted and funny to dark and serious.
*   You need an approved character in order to play (see <a href="https://discord.com/channels/1350684304019165347/1350688299160829962" target="_blank"
