Welcome to The Starry Shore
Discover an endless number of worlds to explore at The Starry Shore! A place of respite adrift in the Astral Sea, the Starry Shore's magical barriers keep the typical dangers at bay, providing a sanctuary for any and all travelers who seek it.

Adventure through new and exciting worlds via mysterious portals, or kick back with some friends and enjoy the Shore's amenities. We welcome travelers of all shapes and sizes and from all walks of life, so come let your imagination soar at The Starry Shore!

How Things Work here?

Player Characters (PCs) exist in The Starry Shore, an interplanar hub with the main street leading to many portals. Dungeon Masters create those portals leading to whatever worlds they dream up. PCs can visit those worlds via live sessions and/or text role play (TRP).

PCs earn levels and gold via hours played in live sessions. Characters continue to level past level 20 using the hours-played formula. Though their level will not increase any further, they will gain additional rewards for each level:

  • 21, 22, 23: An Epic Boon each
  • 24: The ability to earn an artifact rarity item for your character
  • 25-30: A +2 increase to one ability score and its maximum.

And if you'd like to see how active the server is check out the session-log channel or the statistics in the announcements channel.

What kind of D&D we play here?

Most of the games offered here are exciting one-shots, perfect for those who love spontaneous adventures. There are a few DMs that host campaigns; typically, they like to play a few sessions with new players to see if there’s a good fit. If they like your gaming style, they might invite you to join a campaign. This server mainly focuses on these one-shot games, which make up the majority of our activities, allowing you to play on your own schedule.

We’re proud to say that our community only includes the coolest players. That said, we embrace diversity in play styles. Whether you’re someone who revels in rich storytelling and immersive roleplay, or a strategist who enjoys optimizing characters to their fullest potential, you’ll find a welcoming space here. Everyone is invited to bring their unique approach to the game.

Modes of Play:

  1. Live Voice Sessions: You gain "hours played", gold, and items as rewards from playing. (We use "hours played" in place of experience for leveling up.) This is the main method of play. And these kind of sessions can be anything, from lighthearted and funny to dark and serious.
  2. Text Roleplay: or TRP, there's 2 types of it:
    • PBP/TRP in DMs worlds: This for casual trp, you can role play whenever you're free and the dm does the same.
    • Trp for fun: This is the trp that happens inside the starry shore (Crossroad category, Starry City category).

Member Responsibilities

  • Have common sense, and follow the Server Code of Conduct.
  • You must be 18 years of age or older to play on the server (This is both for social and legal reasons. Sorry, no exceptions.).
  • PVP requires consent from both parties including the DM. Consent needs to be communicated, not just implied.
  • Sexual themes are not allowed until consent from the whole party is given.
  • Adult themes and language may appear. Our DM worlds range from lighthearted and funny to dark and serious.
  • You need an approved character in order to play (see the getting-started channel).
  • Players must track their characters (level, gold, items, etc) and keep this information updated in the player folder provided, as well as the relevant log channels.
  • In a rules dispute between a DM and Player, the DM’s word goes until the session is over and the staff can mediate.

Unsure of something?

Ask away! Our community is welcoming, knowledgeable, and happy to help.

Open Door Policy

The staff team’s goal is to promote a safe and fun environment for all server members. You are invited to contact any staff through voice or private messages with questions or concerns as you see fit.
