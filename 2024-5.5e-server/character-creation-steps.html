Creation Tips

  • Use one of two base equipment options:
    • Starting equipment granted by class and background
    • Max values for the starting gold rolls of your 1st class
  • Average hp, not rolled. Take the maximum HP for first level and the average HP for all levels after
  • Multiclassing is allowed
  • Background customization is allowed
  • You can use any content listed in Allowed Materials
Note: All new characters begin with a special Sending Stone, as specified below under the Magic Items section of the rules.

Character Slots

  • Each Player starts with 3 character slots.
  • For every character that reaches Tier 3 (level 11), you gain +1 additional slot, up to a maximum of 3 level up slots.
  • Full DMs receive one additional character slot.
  • Server Supporters also gain extra slots: Steward: +1, Heir: +2, Noble: +4.
