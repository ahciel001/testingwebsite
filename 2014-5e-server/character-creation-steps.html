Creation Tips

  • Equipment Options: Use one of two base options:
    • Starting equipment granted by class and background
    • Max values for the starting gold rolls of your 1st class
  • Hit Points: Average HP, not rolled. Take the maximum HP for 1st level and the average HP for all levels after.
  • Multiclassing: Allowed.
  • Backgrounds: Customization is allowed.
  • Sources: You can use any content listed in Allowed Materials.
Note: All new characters begin with a special Sending Stone, as specified below under the Magic Items section of the rules.
